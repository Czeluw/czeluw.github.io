White Space

Ebay

Ebay's Sales

Ebat makes great use of white spaces to highlight breaks between sections, and atract the attention of the user to specific deals. Ebay also uses a slight use of contrast, but focuses most of their design on white space. A simple yet efficient approach.

Contrast

Pandora

Music Steaming

As opposed to Ebay, Pandora takes full advantage of the contrast effect. Since their services are of more limited functionality, the company uses a simple base colors along with abrupt tone swaps to highlight special advertisement and create an easy-to-read interface.

Alignment

TwitchTv

Gaming Streaming

Twitch has become a popular avenue for gaming streaming. And like many other popular video streaming service (such as Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, etc) they have created a left and right horizontal aligment for a simplified browsing. Users are able to use horizontal rows with the items of equal size that can be experience both in the desktop and mobile versions of the website.
